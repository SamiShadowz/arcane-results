FAQ

Common Questions

What if I'm not sure which service I need?
That's exactly what the strategy call is for. We'll talk through where you are, where you want to go, and I'll recommend the right starting point. No pressure to buy anything on the call.
Do I need a GoHighLevel account?
If we're building in GHL, yes — you'll need an active account. If you don't have one yet, I'll help you choose the right plan during our strategy call. I also build on WordPress and custom platforms if that's a better fit.
Do you write the copy or do I need to provide it?
I write all funnel copy, email sequences, and page content. If you have existing copy you love, I'll work with that. Either way, you approve everything before it goes live.
How long does a typical project take?
A Strategic Growth Blueprint takes 3–5 days. A funnel build takes 5–10 days. A full revenue system takes 2–3 weeks. Every project starts with a clear timeline so you know exactly what to expect.
What if I'm not tech-savvy?
That's the whole point. I build the system, record a walkthrough video so you understand how it works, and make sure you're never stuck. You don't need to be technical — you just need to know your business.
Can you integrate with my existing tools?
Yes — Stripe, PayPal, Calendly, Kajabi, Teachable, Zapier, and most major platforms. If you're not sure whether your stack works, just ask on the strategy call.
What's your refund policy?
I scope every project before you pay so there are no surprises. If at any point during the build you feel it's not the right fit, we'll discuss it directly. Full details are in my Terms of Service.
